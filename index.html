Hi!

I'm Jackson Rushing. I'm a Game Development and Entrepreneurship student at UOIT.

I love programming games, and I've made a couple, check them out!

R.E.D. was my project for the first semester of first year. It's a console based puzzle game with a ton of levels as well as a level editor. check it out here!

SYF was my project for the second semester of first year. It's an arena based twin stick shooter that competed at a student games showcase, Level Up 2016 and won best first year game at UOIT's Gamecon 2016. Check it out here!

In second year, I worked on a local multiplayer game called Bustle. It competed at Level Up 2016, and is available for free on itch.io. Check it out here!

In between second year and third year, I worked on a VR game called Monday Night Mines. It was built for UOIT to show at university fairs to demonstrate the kind of things we do in the Game Development program - check out a trailer here!

During third year, I worked for a professor developing tools to teach first & second years concepts like vector math, 3D transformations, and physics.

I'm most experienced with gameplay programming, but I have a lot of experience developing tools and Unity extensions. Here's an example of a tool I made to help our artists place waypoints while creating tracks for a racing game I'm currently working on.
